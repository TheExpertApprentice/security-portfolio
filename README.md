## About This Repository

This repository documents a quarterly cybersecurity tabletop exercise
programme that I designed, facilitated, and reported on as Simulation Lead.

The exercise tested our team's ability to detect, triage, and respond to
a simulated network intrusion — specifically, unusual external scanning
activity targeting both an external-facing web portal and an internal
database system simultaneously.

The full report is available here: [View Report (PDF)](To be uploaded soon)

## My Role & Contributions

I was responsible for the end-to-end delivery of this exercise:

- **Scenario Design** — Built the inject around a dual-system scan to
  deliberately test whether the team would prioritise the internal system
  threat over the more visible external one.
- **Facilitation** — Led the 90-minute discussion-based session with
  participants from Security Operations, Development, and Infrastructure.
- **Analysis** — Observed and documented responses in real time, identified
  gaps, and developed eight strategic recommendations.
- **Report Authorship** — Produced the formal audit-ready report, including
  a tracked risk register with owners and target dates.

## Organisational Impact

| Outcome | Impact | Status |
|---|---|---|
| Firewall misconfiguration identified and remediated | Closed a critical vulnerability exposing an internal system to external traffic | Completed Q2 2026 |
| SOAR implementation scoped | Automated containment of threats — no manual intervention required | In Progress Q3 2026 |
| Fail2ban deployed on all critical servers | Real-time automated blocking of brute-force and scanning activity | Completed Q2 2026 |
| WAF deployment approved | Application-layer protection for external-facing portal | Planned Q3 2026 |
| Bastion host architecture approved | Eliminated direct external admin access to internal servers | Planned Q3 2026 |
| IR framework adopted as team standard | Consistent, step-by-step incident response regardless of who is on shift | Adopted Q2 2026 |
| Risk register updated with 8 tracked items | Formal accountability with owners and target dates | Ongoing |
